# Pipes & Counting

Pipes (`|`) allow you to take the output of one command and use it as the input for another.

In this directory, there is a file called `data.txt`. Your goal is to count how many times the word **"banana"** appears in the file.

The flag is the **number** of occurrences.

## Hint:

Use `grep` to find the lines, and pipe the result into `wc` to count them.

## Allowed commands:

- grep
- wc
